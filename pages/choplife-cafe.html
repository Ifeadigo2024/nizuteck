Web Platform

Choplife Café

A scalable food ordering platform designed with clean UX, structured frontend logic, and reliable payment workflows.

Context

Choplife Café needed a digital ordering system that could support increasing traffic, manage orders reliably, and remain easy to extend as the business scaled.

Approach

The platform was designed with a modular frontend structure, separating UI concerns from data and logic to ensure clarity, maintainability, and long-term growth.

Key Decisions

  • Component-based frontend architecture
  • Clear separation between cart, checkout, and payment logic
  • Performance-first loading and state management

Outcome

The result is a stable, scalable system that supports current business needs while remaining adaptable for future features, integrations, and operational growth.
